Meet Our Professor

This site is dedicated to quality mathematics education.

Professor Leonard

Professor

My name is Professor Leonard. I have a passion to teach the world mathematics. Not just mathematics in a trivial, meaningless way, but to teach people who want to learn mathematics at an extremely thorough, in-depth level. My goal is to create topical videos that cover the whole spectrum of mathematics. This of course takes time and money. With every bit of support I receive, I plan, execute, and deliver new videos that are shared with the world for free. Help me make mathematics education better!!! Help me help students whose math teachers have "given up" and/or don't want to be there in the first place. Together we can shift the paradigm of mathematics education in this world. We can create a robust and rigorous education experience that is accessible and, hopefully, even fun! Lets change some minds about math.... Thank you so much for taking the time to read this and I hope to earn your support!
